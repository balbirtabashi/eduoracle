WHAT WE DO

The standard chunk of Lorem Ipsum

Eduoracle integrates the different entities of educational ecosystem - School/Colleges, Teachers, Students and Vendors to ensure authentic and holistic exchange of useful information amongst these entities. We mentor and assist schools/colleges, students, teachers and vendors in realising their various academic and business needs. Eduoracle endeavours to minimize the cost of online marketing/promotion and branding, and at the same time ensures that small schools, colleges, knowledge resource centres, students and teachers in remote locations are not devoid of authentic and useful information.

  • 1. Authentic and holistic information to students/parents about prospective Schools and Colleges, linkage to reputed teachers, buying notes mechanism from teachers/institutes/coaching centres across India.
  • 2. Zero Cost branding/advertisement/promotional tool to the Schools and Colleges, teachers, students and vendors.
  • 3. An opportunity for Schools and Colleges to put their teachers/vendors requirements without any hassle.
  • 4. A web based application completely free of cost to teachers who are looking for private tuitions, jobs in Schools and Colleges, and for selling their notes to students across India.
  • 5. Products/Services Listing for Vendors catering to education sector.
